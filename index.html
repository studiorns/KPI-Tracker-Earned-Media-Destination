Key Insights

Earned Media metrics show strong performance through August 2025. Sentiment continues to excel at 62.9% vs 37.6% forecast (167.5% achievement). Despite being slightly below forecast targets, the consistent growth trajectory and exceptional sentiment performance demonstrate strong earned media engagement.
